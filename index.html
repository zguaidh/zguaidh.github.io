About

Blood is a vital resource, yet many hospitals face shortages, leading to critical situations that can endanger lives. The idea for Life Giver was born out of the urgent need to streamline the blood donation process and make it more accessible to everyone.

This project is a Portfolio Project for ALX Africa. For more information, visit ALX Africa.
